Frequently Asked Questions

What games are supported on Shadow Mods?
We support all major PC games that allow modding, including Skyrim, Fallout 4, Minecraft, GTA V, Stardew Valley, and many more. Our catalog is continuously expanding based on community demand.
How do I install mods?
You can install mods manually by downloading and following included instructions, or use our Shadow Mod Manager for automatic one-click installation and updates.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Can I request a refund?
Yes, we offer a 14-day refund policy if a mod doesn't work as described or causes compatibility issues with your game.
How do I become a content creator?
Simply sign up for a creator account, verify your identity, and submit your first mod for review. Our team will guide you through the process.
